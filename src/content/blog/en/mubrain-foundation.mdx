# MuBrain Foundation

MuBrain is being rebuilt as a knowledge-oriented website.

The purpose of this site is not only to publish articles, but to structure knowledge in a way that can be maintained in GitHub, deployed with Astro, and progressively aligned with an OKF-inspired model.

This first article is intentionally simple. It stabilizes the content architecture before the full MuBrain structure is introduced.

The next step will be to separate the public website content from the internal knowledge base, while keeping both understandable by humans and usable by AI agents.
