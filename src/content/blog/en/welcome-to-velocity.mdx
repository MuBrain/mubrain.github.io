# MuBrain Foundation

MuBrain is being rebuilt as a knowledge-oriented website designed for both humans and AI agents.

The goal is not only to publish pages, but to structure knowledge in a way that can be maintained in GitHub, deployed with Astro, and progressively aligned with an OKF-inspired knowledge model.

This first article is temporary. It exists to stabilize the Velocity blog collection while the MuBrain content architecture is being prepared.
